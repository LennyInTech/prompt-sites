How to adapt this starter

Use the structure below as a checklist while you rename and customize the sections.

  1. 1. Define your site
    Replace this intro with a brief description: who the site is for and what it offers. Keep it concise and specific.
    Audience Main outcome
  2. 2. Customize the hero
    Rewrite the main heading, subtitle, and primary action to match your product, service, or idea. Emphasize one primary goal for visitors.
    Clear value prop Primary CTA
  3. 3. Explain the details
    Use the features section to describe what you offer and how it helps. Aim for 3–5 short points rather than long paragraphs.
    Benefits, not just features
  4. 4. Add a clear next step
    In the contact area, decide what you want visitors to do: email you, book a call, join a list, or something else. Make it explicit and easy.
