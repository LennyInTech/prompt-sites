Is using Spark considered cheating?

Not at all! Spark is a coach, not a cheat sheet. It helps you understand concepts and organize your time so you can do the work yourself. We don't write essays for you; we help you brainstorm ideas.

Can I use it for any subject?

Absolutely. From Calculus to History to French, Spark's AI is trained on a massive library of high school and early college curriculums.

Does it work on my phone?

Yes! Spark is designed mobile-first so you can study on the bus, in the library, or from the comfort of your bed (we won't judge).

Can I cancel the Pro plan anytime?

Yup. No contracts, no weird hidden fees. Cancel with one click in your settings if you don't need it anymore.
